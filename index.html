BUBBLE BUDS

To start creating just click on the canvas and start typing!!

In order to change the background color, message color and font, use your mouse (or trackpad) to click on the sketch.

The Enter (or return) key will pause/start the program and freeze/unfreeze your message.

Pressing the Left button will clear your message and return the sketch to a blank white canvas.

Pressing the Right button will slowly step through the program while it is paused
